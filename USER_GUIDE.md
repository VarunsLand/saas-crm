# User Manual

Welcome to your SaaS CRM! This guide will help you navigate your new operational hub.

## 1. Creating a Lead
A Lead is a potential client.
1. Click the **Quick Action (+)** button in the bottom right.
2. Select **Add Lead**.
3. Fill in their Name, Phone, and Email.
4. They will now appear in your Pipeline.

## 2. Moving the Pipeline Stage
The Pipeline is your visual sales board.
1. Navigate to **Pipeline** in the sidebar.
2. Click and hold a lead card.
3. Drag it across the columns (e.g., from *Contacted* to *Proposal*).
4. Drop it to instantly save the new stage.

## 3. Converting a Lead to a Customer
When a lead says "Yes" and pays:
1. Open the lead's profile.
2. Change their status to **WON**.
3. The system will automatically convert them into a **Customer** and move them to the Customers page.

## 4. Adding Revenue
Track your income against specific clients.
1. Go to the **Revenue** tab or use the **Quick Action (+)**.
2. Select the customer who paid.
3. Enter the amount and a description (e.g., "Web Design Deposit").

## 5. Adding an Expense
Track your outgoing operational costs.
1. Go to the **Expenses** tab.
2. Click **Add Expense**.
3. Choose a category (e.g., Software, Marketing) and enter the amount.

## 6. Creating a Task
Never forget to follow up!
1. Go to the **Tasks** tab or use the **Quick Action (+)**.
2. Title the task (e.g., "Send Contract").
3. Assign it to a Lead or Customer, pick a due date, and save.

## 7. Viewing Reports
Understand your business health.
1. Go to the **Dashboard**.
2. View your total revenue, expense, and net profit at the top.
3. Scroll down to see interactive charts visualizing your growth and profit trends over the last 6 months.
